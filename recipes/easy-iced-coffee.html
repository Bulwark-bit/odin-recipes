Easy Iced Coffee

Description

Learn how to make iced coffee with this iced coffee recipe! We use instant coffee in this cheater-version of an iced cappuccino that's very easy to make, and very nice to drink!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons warm water
  • 2 teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 6 fluid ounces cold milk

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Combine warm water, instant coffee, and sugar in a sealable jar. Seal and shake until foamy.

  3. Pour into a glass full of ice; add milk. Adjust to taste if necessary.
